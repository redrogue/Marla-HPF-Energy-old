FABRICATED ASSEMBLIES

At HPF we can provide an array of complex services using established and proven techniques and equipment to provide economical piping solutions for any project.

Over the last 40 years we have been involved in the supply of pre-fabricated piping spools & vessels for numerous clients in the offshore Oil & Gas industries worldwide. We have developed our expertise in this field through utilising our managed supply chain of accredited material suppliers in conjunction with engaging only world-class fabrication facilities for final production.

Our extensive experience gives us the ability to procure, supply, manufacture, fabricate and weld a wide range of metals. Including:

  • Carbon Steels (including Low Temperature and High Yield strength grades)
  • Nickel 200 & 201
  • Nickel Alloys 400, 600 series, 800 series & 825
  • Hastelloy C276, C22, C2000, B2, B3, G30 & X
  • Cupro Nickel Alloys
  • Titanium Grades 1, 2, 3, 5 & 7
  • Zirconium 702
  • Tantalum
  • Aluminium
  • Duplex Stainless Steels [UNS 31803 etc]
  • Super Duplex Stainless Steels [UNS 32760 etc]
  • Alloys 904L, 2RE69, 254 SMo etc
  • 300 Series Stainless Steel [304, 316, 321 & 317L etc]
